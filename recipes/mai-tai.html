Mai Tai

Description

You just stepped onto a beach in the middle of the Carribean. Only one thing is missing...your drink! Not for long if you follow this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Spiced Rum
  • Coconut Rum
  • Grenadine
  • Pineapple Juice
  • Orange Juice

Directions:

  1. Combine Spiced Rum, Coconut Rum, Pineapple Juice and Orange Juice in a Shaker
  2. Fill a Glass with Ice and Pour the Mixture on Top
  3. Pour Grenadine on Top
