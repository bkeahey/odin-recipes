Red Sangria

Description

The best red sangria you will ever have!

Ingredients:

  • Spanish Red Wine
  • Triple Sec
  • Brandy
  • Orange Juice
  • Lemon Juice
  • Lemons
  • Apples
  • Limes
  • Sugar
  • Maraschino Cherries
  • Carbonated Water

Directions:

  1. Pour Wine in Pitcher
  2. Pour Triple Sec, Brandy, Orange Juice and Lemon Juice in Pitcher
  3. Pour Fruit and Sugar in Pitcher
  4. Pour Carbonared Water in Pitcher
  5. Mix
